FAQs

I'm new to this. How do I use the web app?
Register as a new user and login using your email and password.
How do I create a Todo?
Go to the homepage, click new and start filling in the details of your Todo.
I'm not satisifed with the functionalities. How do I leave a feedback?
Go to the homepage, click feedback and start filling in the details of your feedback. Keep in mind that we will respond within 3 working days so we appreciate your patience.
Is it possible to make edits to a Todo I already created, without deleting it and making a new one?
Yes of course. When you view a created Todo, you can have the option to edit by clicking edit and filling in the new details.
